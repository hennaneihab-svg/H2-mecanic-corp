Who We Are

About H2 Mecanic Corp

H2 Mecanic Corp is a locally owned automotive repair shop based in Grande Prairie, Alberta. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we've built our reputation on honesty, straightforward pricing, and quality workmanship — treating every vehicle that comes through our doors as if it were our own.

15+

Years of Trusted Service

From routine oil changes to full engine replacements, our team has the experience to diagnose and repair all makes and models — cars, trucks, SUVs, and commercial vehicles alike.

Our Principles

Our Values

Everything we do is guided by a simple set of principles that put the customer first and the work second to none.

Honesty & Transparency

We give you a clear explanation of what's wrong, what it costs, and why it matters. No unnecessary upsells, no pressure — just straight answers so you can make informed decisions about your vehicle.

Quality Workmanship

Our technicians take pride in doing the job right the first time. We use quality parts, follow manufacturer specifications, and pay careful attention to detail on every repair — large or small.

Customer-First Service

We know that getting your vehicle repaired can be stressful. That's why we keep things simple — flexible scheduling, clear communication, and a willingness to work with you every step of the way.

Certified Expertise

We're a certified CVIP inspection station and qualified to perform insurance and pre-purchase inspections. Our credentials mean your vehicle is in capable, trusted hands.

The Difference

What Sets Us Apart

Full Range of Services Under One Roof Tires, oil changes, suspension, brakes, engine and transmission work, and certified inspections — no need to drive across town for different shops.

Certified CVIP Inspection Station We're authorized to perform Commercial Vehicle Inspection Program inspections, keeping your fleet compliant with Alberta Transportation standards.

Custom Tire Orders — Fast Delivery Can't find the tire size you need? We can special-order any size through our supplier network, with most orders arriving within 1–2 business days.

Proudly Serving Grande Prairie & Area We're a local business built on local trust. Our customers come from Grande Prairie, Clairmont, Sexsmith, Beaverlodge, and all across the Peace Region.
