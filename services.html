Tire Services

Installation, balancing, and custom orders for all vehicle types

Tire Installation & Balancing

Whether you're swapping to winter tires or outfitting your vehicle with a brand-new set, we provide professional mounting and wheel balancing for all vehicle types — from compact cars to heavy-duty trucks. Proper balancing eliminates vibrations, reduces uneven wear, and extends the life of your tires. We carry a range of popular sizes in stock and can source virtually any tire through our supplier network.

Suspension Work

A worn-out suspension affects your ride comfort, handling, and tire wear. We diagnose and repair all suspension components — shocks, struts, ball joints, tie rod ends, control arms, and sway bar links. Whether your vehicle is pulling to one side, bouncing excessively, or making clunking noises over bumps, our team will identify the problem and get it sorted.

Brakes

Your braking system is the most critical safety feature on your vehicle. We provide complete brake service — from pad and rotor replacement to caliper rebuilds, brake line repairs, and fluid flushes. If you're hearing squealing, grinding, or feeling pulsation when braking, don't wait. We'll inspect your system and give you a clear assessment of what's needed to keep you safe on the road.

Vehicle Inspections

Certified inspections for insurance, commercial vehicles, and private purchases

Vehicle Insurance Inspection

Required by Alberta insurance providers when registering a vehicle from out of province or re-registering a salvage-title vehicle. Our inspection covers the structural integrity, braking system, steering, suspension, lights, glass, and overall safety of the vehicle. You'll receive a signed inspection report that meets provincial requirements.

CVIP Inspection

As a certified Commercial Vehicle Inspection Program (CVIP) station, we perform mandatory annual inspections for commercial trucks, trailers, buses, and emergency vehicles. Our inspection follows all Alberta Transportation standards, covering the frame, axles, brakes, coupling devices, electrical systems, and more. Stay compliant and keep your fleet on the road.

Vehicle Purchase Inspection

Buying a used vehicle? A pre-purchase inspection can save you thousands. We provide a thorough mechanical assessment — checking the engine, transmission, suspension, brakes, exhaust, electrical systems, and fluid conditions — so you know exactly what you're getting into before you sign.
